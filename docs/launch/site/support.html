Support

Stuck on something, hit a bug, or want to ask a question? Here's how to reach a human.

Before you email — most common stuff

  • How does the streak work?
  • How do hints work?
  • When do my hearts refill?
  • Why didn't I get a notification?

Full FAQ has more.

Report a bug

Email [YOUR_FEEDBACK_EMAIL] with as much of this as you can manage:

  • iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • iPhone model (Settings → General → About)
  • Pictok version (Settings → Pictok, or "Version" inside the app)
  • What you did right before it broke
  • What happened that shouldn't have
  • Whether you can reproduce it
  • Screenshot or screen recording if relevant

Response time: I aim for 24–48 hours. If it's been a week and you haven't heard back, please poke me again — it probably got buried.

Suggest a feature

Same email — subject "Pictok feature idea". I read everything. I won't promise to build everything.

What's likely to land:

  • Quality-of-life polish (animation feel, copy tweaks, accessibility)
  • Stat/streak improvements
  • Share card variants

What's unlikely (in the spirit of keeping Pictok minimal):

  • Accounts, login, cross-device sync (the local-only philosophy is central)
  • In-app purchases / premium tier
  • Endless/practice mode (the daily-only ritual is the design)
  • Notifications and quests beyond the one daily reminder

Suggest a puzzle

Email [YOUR_FEEDBACK_EMAIL] with the emoji string and the answer. The format we use:

Emoji:        🐝🍃
Answer:       BELIEF
Category:     Song | Movie | Book | Brand | Celeb
Subcategory:  John Mayer · 2006
Reasoning:    bee + leaf → "be-lief"

If we ship your puzzle, you'll get a credit on this page and inside the app.

Complaint about a puzzle

Some puzzles will feel unfair. Sometimes that's the user (it happens to everyone). Sometimes that's the puzzle (it happens to us too). If a puzzle felt arbitrary, culturally narrow, ambiguous, or just plain wrong, email [YOUR_FEEDBACK_EMAIL] with:

  • The puzzle number (visible on the share card or result screen)
  • What about it felt off
  • What answer you tried

We pull puzzles that don't hold up under review.

Delete my data

Pictok doesn't store any of your data on a server — there's nothing for us to delete. To remove the data Pictok stores on your device, delete the app from your phone. If you also have an iCloud backup that included Pictok's data, you can disable iCloud restore for Pictok in iOS Settings.
